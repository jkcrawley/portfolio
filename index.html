The Game is Always Changing

When I first began going to college for Web Development and Programming developers were using technologies and methods that are now dated. Layouts were created with floats and Flash was still big and in demand. Wordpress grew into being the premier CMS, React became a priority framework, and the field is ever evolving.

I am persistent in finding the latest trends in design. I pay attention to what other developers are doing, what they're saying, what technologies is changing the game. Finding ways to build websites faster, more dynamic, and most of all, more efficient. This is why I love what I do, because there is always new challenge!
