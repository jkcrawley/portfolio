Welcome!

I’m James and I’m an aspiring web developer whom was born and raised in Waterloo, Iowa. I started my journey into web development in my early 20s with the first website I built on Angelfire for me and my gamer friends. What began as a smal6l hobby became a passion, so I began going to school in 2008 at Hawkeye College.

At Hawkeye I learned all of the basic: HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I also learned software that is now dated, but was once considered as being prominent in the field such as Adobe Flash and Dreamweaver. I left after my second semester and returned once responsive design started to become essential to developers. In my time at Hawkeye I also learned back-end languages, the most of which I'm proficient in are PHP and MySql.

I had already had a family by the time I began my journey, so it was a challenge balancing my life between working a full time job, caring for my family, and being a full time student/web developer. I completed my Associates in Web Development and Programming in 2018 and have continued to learn things that even my school didn’t teach and master the skills that they did.

My dream is to land my first Web Developer job and finally achieve the career that I’ve been craving for and worked so hard to build towards over the course of a decade. I want to set an example for my children that as long as you’re persistent and never give up you can achieve anything!
